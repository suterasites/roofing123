About This Service

Roof damage doesn't fix itself

A damaged roof only gets worse over time. What starts as a small crack or loose ridge cap can quickly turn into water damage, mould and structural problems. The sooner you act, the less it costs.

We handle everything from minor leak repairs through to full storm damage restoration. Whether it's a tile roof, metal roof or colorbond, we diagnose the problem accurately and fix it properly so it stays fixed.

What's Included

Expert repairs for every roof type

Leak Detection & Repair

We track down the source of the leak, not just where water appears, and fix it at the root cause.

Ridge Capping & Repointing

Cracked or missing ridge capping is repointed and sealed to prevent water entry along the roof ridge.

Tile & Metal Roof Repairs

Cracked, broken or displaced tiles replaced. Metal roofing patched, resealed and restored to weatherproof condition.

Flashing Repairs

Worn or lifted flashing around chimneys, walls and roof penetrations is repaired and sealed watertight.

Storm Damage Restoration

Fast response to storm, hail and wind damage. Emergency tarping available, followed by full permanent repairs.

Why It Matters

Delay costs more than the repair

Prevents Water Damage

Even a small roof leak can cause significant water damage to ceilings, walls and electrical wiring if left unaddressed.

Structural Integrity

Your roof is a structural component of your home. Damaged areas weaken the entire system and can lead to costly frame repairs.

Home Protection

A sound roof protects everything underneath it. Timely repairs keep your family, belongings and property safe from the elements.
