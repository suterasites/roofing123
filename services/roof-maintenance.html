About This Service

A well-maintained roof lasts decades longer

Sydney weather is tough on roofs. Harsh sun, heavy rain and coastal winds take their toll over time. Without regular maintenance, small issues like cracked tiles, lifted flashing and moss buildup turn into costly repairs.

Our roof maintenance service covers everything from routine inspections and cleaning through to moss removal and care around solar panel installations. We help you stay ahead of problems and keep your roof performing at its best.

What's Included

Complete roof care, covered

Routine Inspections

Thorough visual inspections to catch cracked tiles, lifted flashing and early signs of wear before they worsen.

Preventative Care

Proactive maintenance to address minor issues early. Tighten fixings, reseal joints and replace damaged components.

Roof Cleaning

High-pressure and soft wash cleaning to remove dirt, lichen and staining. Restores appearance and prevents degradation.

Moss Removal

Safe removal of moss and lichen growth that traps moisture, damages tiles and compromises your roof's waterproofing.

Solar Panel Area Care

Careful maintenance around solar panels, including clearing debris buildup and checking roof integrity beneath installations.

Why It Matters

Small investment now, big savings later

Extends Roof Life

Regular maintenance can add 10 to 15 years to your roof's lifespan. A small, regular investment saves you from a full replacement down the track.

Prevents Costly Repairs

Catching a cracked tile or loose flashing early costs a fraction of repairing the water damage it would eventually cause.

Maintains Property Value

A clean, well-maintained roof is one of the first things buyers and valuers notice. It signals a well-cared-for property.

Our Process

Methodical, thorough, proactive

01

Full Roof Inspection

02
