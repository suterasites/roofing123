About Us

About Roofing123

Sydney's trusted team for gutter cleaning, roof maintenance and repairs. Built on honest work, fair pricing and doing the job right the first time.

Our Story

Quality roofing care
across Sydney

Roofing123 was founded by Mouhmad with a straightforward goal - to provide Sydney homeowners with roofing and gutter care they can actually rely on. No inflated quotes, no cutting corners, no leaving the job half done. Just solid, honest work from a team that takes pride in getting it right.

From blocked gutters in the Eastern Suburbs to storm-damaged ridge capping out west, we have worked across every part of Sydney. We understand the challenges that local weather throws at roofs, from summer storms and heavy rain to coastal salt air and falling branches. That experience means we know what to look for and how to fix it properly.

Whether it is a routine gutter clean, a full roof inspection or an urgent leak repair, we treat every job with the same care. We show up on time, we communicate clearly and we leave the site clean. It sounds simple because it should be.
